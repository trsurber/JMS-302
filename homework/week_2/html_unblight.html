What is UnBlight?

Blight:the state or result of being blighted or deteriorated; dilapidation; decay

Many of America's once great cities have suffered recently as people move in masses to the suburbs, leaving behind empty houses and streets.

UnBlight brings together journalists, policymakers, advocates, and students to search for ways to combat blight.

Mercer University, the Center for Collaborative Journalism (CCJ), and the Sunlight Foundation are hosting Unblight.
